● AI Controller — Double DQN

9-dimensional state: queue lengths + phase encoding + timer
Neural network: 128 → 128 hidden units (ReLU)
Double DQN: policy selects, target evaluates
Experience replay buffer (10,000 transitions)
ε-greedy exploration with annealing
Adapts green time to real-time queue demand

● Traditional Controller — Fixed Timer

Fixed 30-second cycle per direction
Sequential rotation: N → S → E → W
No adaptation to traffic conditions
Baseline approach used at most Indian intersections
Equal time regardless of queue length
Wastes green time on empty approaches
